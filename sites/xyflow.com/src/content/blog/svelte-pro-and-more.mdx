We’ve been working hard this spring, and today we’re introducing Svelte Pro, alongside a
wave of major updates across the platform, including a new, fully integrated Pro
experience, long-awaited Dark Mode, and countless improvements make everything more
cohesive, and smoother to use.

### Introducing Svelte Flow Pro

We’ve expanded our Pro offering to the Svelte ecosystem, with a brand new set of Pro
examples, plus beefed up guides and documentation.

The Svelte community continues to impress us, and we’re excited to support it with more
in-depth, production-ready examples to help you build bigger and more complex apps.

We love seeing what you make with Svelte Pro. If you've built something you are proud of,
feel free to submit your project to our showcase: https://svelteflow.dev/showcase

### The New Pro Platform

We’ve integrated the Pro Platform directly into the site, making it easier than ever to
explore and use Pro content.

Instead of feeling like a separate experience, Pro is now woven into the the site. You can
access Pro examples, guides, and resources across both React Flow and Svelte Flow without
friction.

### Dark Mode (Finally)

A long awaited sight for sore eyes: Dark Mode now available across the site and examples.

We’ve implemented full Dark Mode support across the entire site and all examples. Along
the way, we've given the site a face lift, improving color consistency, refining
gradients, and making accessibility upgrades to ensure everything looks and feels right in
both themes.

If you’ve been waiting for a more comfortable viewing experience, especially during
late-night sessions, this one’s for you.

### And more

Alongside the big updates, we’ve made a wide range of improvements across the platform:

**UI & Navigation**

- Cleaner navigation with improved menus
- Better account handling
- Smoother buttons, animations, and transitions
- and a lot more behind the scenes, with major improvements to the codebase that make the
  site easier to navigate and explore.

These updates are all about making the platform more unified, more polished, and more
enjoyable to use.

As always, there’s more to come.
